Personal Preference Enchants

There are some enchants that you should consider yourself before adding them.

Thorns

It makes your armor take more durability when returning damage

Knockback

It makes pvp a bit harder as you will push away your opponent, making it harder to hit them

Punch

It's like the knockback enchantment, but for bows

Curse of Vanishing

Makes your items disappear on death, prevents your enemy from getting more gear but also prevents you from getting back up
